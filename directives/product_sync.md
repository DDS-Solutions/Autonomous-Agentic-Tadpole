# 🔄 Directive: Product Sync (SOP-PRO-05)

## 🎯 Primary Objective
Ensure 100% horizontal alignment between the Engine (Engineering), the Brand (Marketing), and the Vision (Strategy). This directive governs the weekly "Sync Ritual" to prevent siloed development.

---

## 🏗️ Sync Pillars

### 1. Technical Health
- **Source**: `ops_review.md` and `testing_patterns`.
- **Goal**: Communicate core engine breakthroughs or stability risks to the product team.

### 2. Market Pulse
- **Source**: `market_trend_analysis.md` and `user_feedback_analysis.md`.
- **Goal**: Align the upcoming `sprint_planning.md` goals with actual market demand and user friction points.

### 3. Marketing Launch Sync
- **Source**: `campaign_launch.md`.
- **Goal**: Ensure technical assets (benchmarks, screenshots, demos) are ready for the marketing team's release cadence.

---

## 🛠️ Ritual SOP

### 1. Agenda Synthesis
- **Action**: Use Agent 99 to pull summaries from all major reports (`Financial`, `Security`, `Engineering`) generated during the week.

### 2. Decision Logging
- **Output**: `PRODUCT_SYNC_[ID].md`.
- **Focus**: Clear "Action Items" assigned to specific departments or agent clusters.

---

## 📊 Alignment Velocity
Monitor the "Speed-to-Implementation" of items identified during the sync. High alignment velocity is achieved when feedback from the market is reflected in a production release within 14 days.
