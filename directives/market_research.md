## 🎯 Primary Objective
Deliver actionable intelligence on target markets, niche opportunities, and user sentiment. This directive ensures that product development is driven by a "Market-First" philosophy.

## 🏗️ Research Methodology

### 1. Trend Analysis
- **Tool**: `search_web`
- **Action**: Research the top 5 emerging trends in AI Agent orchestration (e.g., "MCP adoption," "Local-First Swarms").
- **Metric**: Identify "Signal Strength" (frequency of mention across dev forums).

### 2. Audience Mapping
- **Goal**: Identify where the users congregate.
- **Focus**: Subreddits, Discord servers, and GitHub organizations relevant to the research topic.

### 3. Gap Analysis
- **Execution**: Compare current top-tier offerings against the identified user "Pain Points."
- **Output**: Identify 3 specific "Standout Features" that Tadpole OS can implement to capture market share.
