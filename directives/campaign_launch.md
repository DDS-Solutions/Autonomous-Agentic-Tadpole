# 🚀 Directive: Campaign Launch (SOP-MKT-01)

## 🎯 Primary Objective
Orchestrate a high-impact product or feature launch. This directive ensures that marketing assets, audience segmentation, and performance tracking are aligned for maximum market penetration.

---

## 📅 Pre-Launch Strategy

### 1. Market Insights
- **Tool**: `market_research.md` & `competitive_audit.md`
- **Action**: Identify top 3 market gaps and align the campaign "Hook" to these deficiencies.

### 2. Audience Segmentation
- **Goal**: Define "Target Cluster."
- **Focus**: High-intent users, early adopters, and industry influencers.

### 3. Asset Synthesis
- **Deliverables**: 
  - Campaign Landing Page (SEO Hardened).
  - Social Media Pulse (Twitter/X, LinkedIn content).
  - Newsletter Bullet (Deep-dive into technical "Standout").

---

## 🚀 Execution Phase

### 1. Quality Gate
- **Check**: Verify all links and CTAs (Call to Action).
- **Compliance**: Ensure branding adheres to the `IDENTITY.md` standards.

### 2. Pulse Monitoring
- **Metrics**: 
  - Click-Through Rate (CTR).
  - Conversion Density.
  - Social Sentiment Velocity.

---

## 📊 Post-Launch Analysis
Update the `engagement_report.md` with final conversion data. Use the findings to optimize the next `free_launch_plan.md` iteration.
