# GEO Fundamentals

**Optimize for the answer, not the click.**

## What Works
- **Original Data**: Stats, quotes.
- **Structure**: Tables, Lists, FAQs.
- **Definitions**: Clear, extractable.

---

## 🧠 Aletheia Reasoning Protocol (AI Visibility)

### 1. Generator (Question Mapping)
*   **User Intent**: "Asking questions, not verifying keywords.".
*   **Data Points**: "What unique stat can we provide?".
*   **Structure**: "FAQ vs Table vs List".

### 2. Verifier (Extraction Test)
*   **The "Ctrl+F" Test**: "Can a bot easily find the answer?".
*   **Context**: "Is the answer buried in a metaphor?".
*   **Authority**: "Why should the AI trust this number?".

### 3. Reviser (Structuring)
*   **Schema**: "Wrap this in JSON-LD".
*   **Directness**: "Move the answer to the first sentence".
